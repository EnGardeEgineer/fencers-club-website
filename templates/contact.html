Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a tour?

You can schedule a tour by calling us at (555) 123-4567 or by filling out the contact form above. Tours are available Monday through Friday between 10 AM and 4 PM.

What are your parking options?

We have a dedicated parking lot for members and visitors. Street parking is also available in the surrounding area.

Do you offer equipment rental?

Yes, we provide basic equipment rental for beginners and visitors. Members are encouraged to purchase their own equipment as they progress.

What should I bring to my first lesson?

For your first lesson, just bring comfortable athletic clothing and sneakers. We provide all necessary equipment for beginners, including masks, jackets, and weapons.
