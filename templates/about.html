Welcome to Fencers Club

Fencers Club is a not-for-profit organization dedicated to the pursuit of excellence through the sport of fencing. We actively support a culture of sharing by performing community services that extend beyond fencing.

Located in the heart of Manhattan, our club has been training champions and building character through the noble art of fencing for over 140 years.
