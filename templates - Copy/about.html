Our History

Founded in 1984, Fencers Club has been a cornerstone of the fencing community for over 40 years. What began as a small training facility has grown into one of the nation's premier fencing clubs.

Our journey has been marked by numerous achievements, including training Olympic athletes, hosting national championships, and developing innovative training programs that have shaped the future of fencing.

Today, we continue to build on our legacy while embracing modern training methods and technology to provide the best possible experience for our members.
